# #2305 Fix delimiter pairing after partial deletion of "|"

## 2026/03/29 Fix bracket auto-pairing after orphaned pipe

### What
- Updated `math-bracket-open` in `math-edit.scm` to handle orphaned `|` delimiters.
- Invalidate pairing context (`set! t #f` / `set! u #f`) when a partially deleted `||` is detected.

### Why
- After deleting one side of `||`, the editor retained an invalid pairing state.
- This caused subsequent delimiters (e.g., `()`) to fail auto-pairing.

### How
- Detect partially deleted `|` pairs during pairing lookup.
- Reset pairing context without modifying the AST.
- Ensures subsequent delimiters are handled as fresh input.
